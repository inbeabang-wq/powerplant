왕복엔진 오버홀 개요

Reciprocating Engine Overhaul

오버홀(Overhaul)은 엔진을 완전 분해·검사·복원해 신품에 준하는 상태로 되돌리는 작업이다.An overhaul is the complete disassembly, inspection, and restoration of an engine to a condition equivalent to new.

시기: TBO(Time Between Overhaul) 도달, 또는 상태 기준(On-condition).Timing: at TBO (time between overhaul), or on-condition.

흐름: 인수검사 → 분해 → 세척 → 검사 → 수리/교환 → 재조립 → 시험.Flow: receiving inspection → disassembly → cleaning → inspection → repair/replace → reassembly → test.

So a mineral spirits solvent is used.

인수검사 · 분해

Receiving Inspection & Disassembly

인수검사(Receiving Inspection): 엔진 외관·기록·운전이력 확인, 손상·누설 점검.Receiving inspection: check the engine's exterior, records, and operating history, and inspect for damage and leaks.

분해(Disassembly)는 정해진 순서·전용공구로 부품 손상 없이 진행.Disassembly is done in the prescribed order with proper tools, without damaging parts.

분해 부품은 식별·정리해 혼입·분실을 방지한다.Disassembled parts are identified and organized to prevent mix-ups and loss.

세척

Cleaning

세척(Cleaning)은 검사 정확도의 전제 — 오염·카본·페인트를 제거한다.Cleaning is the prerequisite for accurate inspection — it removes contaminants, carbon, and paint.

방법: 용제 세척(Solvent), 화학 세척, 그릿/소다 블라스팅(Blasting).Methods: solvent cleaning, chemical cleaning, and grit/soda blasting.

⚠️ 알루미늄 등 연질금속은 손상 방지를 위해 세척법을 선택한다.Warning: for soft metals such as aluminum, choose the cleaning method carefully to avoid damage.

비누는 제거가 어려워 금속 표면/기공에 남아 엔진 운전 시 오일을 오염시키고 심한 발포를 일으킨다.Soap is hard to remove and remains in the surface or pores of the metal, contaminating the engine oil and causing severe foaming when the engine operates.

모범답안: 비누는 제거가 어려워 금속 표면·기공에 남아, 엔진 운전 시 오일을 오염시키고 심한 발포를 일으키기 때문입니다.Soap is difficult to remove and stays in the surface and pores of the metal, contaminating the oil and causing severe foaming during operation.

육안 검사

Visual Inspection

육안검사(Visual Inspection)는 모든 검사의 기본 — 균열·부식·마모·변색·소손을 확인.Visual inspection is the basis of all inspection — checking for cracks, corrosion, wear, discoloration, and burning.

확대경·보어스코프(Borescope)로 내부·접근곤란부를 점검.Use a magnifier or borescope to inspect internal and hard-to-reach areas.

이상 발견 시 정밀검사(NDI)로 확인한다.If a defect is found, confirm it with non-destructive inspection (NDI).

구조 검사 (비파괴검사)

Structural Inspection (NDI)

비파괴검사(NDI: Non-Destructive Inspection)로 표면·내부 결함을 탐지한다.Non-destructive inspection (NDI) detects surface and internal defects.

방법: 자분탐상(Magnetic Particle), 침투탐상(Dye Penetrant), 와전류(Eddy Current), 초음파(Ultrasonic), 방사선(X-ray).Methods: magnetic particle, dye penetrant, eddy current, ultrasonic, and X-ray.

균열(Crack)은 피로파괴의 시작 — 조기 발견이 안전의 핵심.A crack is the start of fatigue failure — early detection is key to safety.

치수 검사

Dimensional Inspection

치수검사(Dimensional Inspection): 마이크로미터·게이지로 마모량을 측정해 한계와 비교.Dimensional inspection: measure wear with micrometers and gauges and compare with the limits.

측정 항목: 실린더 보어(Bore), 피스톤·링 마모, 베어링 간극, 크랭크샤프트 진원도.Items measured: cylinder bore, piston and ring wear, bearing clearance, and crankshaft runout.

서비스 한계(Service limit) 초과 부품은 교환한다.Parts exceeding the service limit are replaced.
