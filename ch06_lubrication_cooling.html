Principles of Engine Lubrication

윤활(Lubrication)의 5대 기능: 마찰감소(Friction reduction), 냉각(Cooling), 기밀(Sealing), 청정(Cleaning), 방청(Corrosion prevention).The five functions of lubrication: friction reduction, cooling, sealing, cleaning, and corrosion prevention.

오일은 금속면 사이에 유막(Oil film)을 만들어 직접 접촉을 막는다.Oil forms an oil film between metal surfaces to prevent direct contact.

유막 종류: 경계윤활(Boundary)·유체윤활(Hydrodynamic).Types of film: boundary lubrication and hydrodynamic lubrication.
